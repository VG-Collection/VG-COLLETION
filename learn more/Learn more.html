Van Gogh Atelier des Lumieres in Paris


Atelier des Lumières reveals the expressive and powerful brushstrokes of the Dutch painter, illuminated by the bold colours of his sunny landscapes and tormented night-time scenes. With total immersion, visitors can explore the inordinate, chaotic and poetic inner world of Van Gogh. The thematic itinerary traces the different stages of the artist’s life, his stays in Paris, Arles, Saint-Rémy-de-Provence and Auvers-sur-Oise. The visitor accompanies Van Gogh from his beginnings to maturity.


This is an extraordinary journey into the heart of the work of Vincent Van Gogh. In this former iron foundry in the east of Paris, every wall, alcove and pillar is set in motion with projections of the artist’s works thanks to Amiex (Art & Music Immersive Experience).


When the music resonates in this monumental building, the projection of thousands of images transforms the place and literally propels the visitor into Van Gogh’s most famous paintings, from Sunflowers to Starry Night – it’s a unique, multi-sensory exhibition.
